Stine Poulsen

Certified Psychotherapist

I'm a Certified Psychotherapist, trained in Gestalttherapi. A form of therapy, which takes its point of departure in the whole human being. The therapy will therefore focus on both thoughts, feelings and body. Gestalttherapi is a form of therapy that supports, the ability to be present in the moment and your connection to yourself and your surroundings.

The Sessions

Together we'll work in the therapy sessions on uncovering and finding new paths, for example in relation to stuck patterns and difficult life situations. We'll find useful methods for you, so your courses of action increase and help you achieve the changes you desire.

In the therapy we'll focus on increasing your self-care, helping you to enterpret yourself and the situations you find yourself in with a gentler view. My therapy sessions use a pendulum approach, svinging back and forth between your current situation, and experiences from your past.

Why Gestalttherapi?

I use this form of therapy because it presents a unique opportunity to increase your awareness of personal patterns, motives and actions. The methods of Gestalttherapi both challenges and supports your own potential and resources. Happiness in life and well-being is often depends on understanding yourself, your needs and how to obtain them.

I believe we can go far by ourselves, but travel even further when we take the journey together.
