Individual Therapy

For adults & youth aged 15+

Individual therapy supports your journey towards unlocking and understanding your inner strenghts. It sharpens your ability to be present in the moment. The therapy helps you obtain a better connection with youself and your surroundings.

Together we'll discover new courses of action, and methods which will help you obtain a better balance, higher satisfaction & more enjoyment in your life.

I have a hollistic approach to therapy, which means that we'll work with all aspects of you; your thoughts, feelings, senses, conditions and expressions.

Confidentiality
