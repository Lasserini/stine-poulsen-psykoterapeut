Couples Therapy

I have a hollistic approach to therapy, which means that the sessions will focus on emotions, body sensations, conditions and expressions.

Couples Therapy seeks to enchance your understanding of eachother, we'll work on (re)discovering og strengthening your love and cohesion - an opportunity to optain a deeper connection and joy in the relationship.

The Couples Therapy sessions provide an opportunity to examine both the minor daily life conflicts, aswell as larger fundemental issues in the relationship.

Confidentiality
